## Why the name "Wessell"? 💬

Contrary to what you might think, it's not derived from or related to my government name.

It was actually derived from a longer form of "Wessell Urdata" (a username I still use in one form or another), which you could guess is a play on name for "we sell your data". This was accompanied by a picture of Mark Zuckerberg, and I used to have that as my profile picture until some time in 2022 (Now it's just ☺️).

In hindsight, I don't think Facebook really sold user data? It's much more profitable keeping the data to themselves and selling a platform for advertisers to serve ads, but the name stuck around, and here we are.
